About Faculty of Computing and Information Technology (FOCS)

year 1972
- Founded as School of Arts and Science (SAS) under Tunku Abdul Rahman Collage (TARC)

year 2013
- Restructured as Faculty of Applied Sciences and Computing (FASC) when TARC was upgraded to Tunku Abdul Rahman University Collage (TARUC)

year 2017
- Faculty of Computing and Information Technology (FOCS) was formed.


We offer programmes that cover all 5 major disciplines of computing which are: 
- Computer Science
- Data Science 
- Software Engineering 
- Information Technology 
- Information Systems

Our strength or areas of expertise:
- Computer vision
- Image processing
- Text analytics
- Web and mobile computing
- Game development
- Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR)
- Software design and development
- Predictive and forecast modelling (big data analytics)
- Network detection and prevention
- Cybersecurity
- Internet of Things (IoT)
- Blockchain
- Mathematical Sciences
- Data Science
